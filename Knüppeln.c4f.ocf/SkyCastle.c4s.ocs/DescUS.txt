Knüppeln.c4s

Someone thought it would be a good idea to build a castle somewhere in the sky.


About Knüppeln:

Choose your champion at the beginning / at each respawn and knüppel your enemies to death!

Controls:
Double W/A/S/D mid air = Double jump
Space = Blocks most spells near you
Q/E/R = Special abilities of your champion

Range indicators:
Some abilities show range indicators when you press down the button. Mouse click to launch the spell within the range!
